The Kids

Harper Drew

Harper was born in 1997 and is the first born of the Drew family. She went to the School of the Holy Child, an all girls catholic school, from 6th grade to 9th grade and then to Brooks School for the rest of highschool. Harper played soccer and lacrosse and was the head of the event planning committee at Brooks. She interned in the marketing department at Pop Sugar and iHeart Radio. She graduated from Elon University in 2019 with a concentration in Strategic Communication and a minor in Business. Harper is currently working for Bloom Farms, a California Cannabis Company, as a Sales Rep. She enjoys traveling, spending time with family and friends and social media in her free time.

Sam Drew

Sam was born in 1999 she is the middle child of the Drew family. She went to the School of the Holy Child, an all girls catholic school, from 6th grade to 12th. She was a competitive swimmer from age 5 to 18. She is currently a junior at the University of Michigan in the School of Information studying Information Analysis and Entrepreneurship. Sam has always had a passion for problem solving. She worked at American Yacht Club for 6 summers starting as a lifeguard and working her way up to Pool Director in 2018 and 2019. She enjoys traveling, spending time with family and friends and playing cards in her free time.

Keegan Drew

Keegan was born in 2001 and is the baby of the Drew family. He went to the Brunswick School from 6th grade through 12th.

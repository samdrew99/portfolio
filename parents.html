Parents

Parker Drew

Parker, originally from Charlotte, North Carolina, grew up with his sister Mim and his parents Bill and Sharli. He graduated from Brown University in 1991 with an AB in American History and Economics. He then earned an MBA in Finance from NYU Stern School of Business in 1998. Parker is married to Alyson Parry who he met at Brown and they have 3 children (Harper, Sam, and Keegan). They settled down in Harrison, New York and have lived in the same house for the past 17 years. Parker had a 25 year long career in commodities trading, marketing and risk management. He worked as a Natural Gas derivatives trader on Wall Street with Merrill, Morgan Stanley and JP Morgan. Parker now provides expert consulting on commodities and serves as a guest lecturer and Adjunct Professor at Tulane University's Freeman School of Business. His life is centered around poker, watching competitive swimming, golf, traveling and preparing his kids for success in their future endeavors.

Aly Drew

Alyson, originally from Barrington, Rhode Island grew up with her sister Jennifer and her parents John and Anne. She graduated from Brown University in 1991 with an AB in American History. She then earned an MBA in Marketing from NYU Stern School of Business in 2006. Alyson is married to Parker Drew who she met at Brown. She worked in Sales at Maidenform INC. and decided to leave the work field to raise their 3 children (Harper, Sam, and Keegan). They settled down in Harrison, New York and have lived in the same house for the past 17 years. In 2011 Alyson, and her business partner Bets founded Nest Inspired Home a classic and contemporary home furnishing store. Nest has been successful thanks to her hard work and dedication for the past 8 years. Her life is centered around her business, watching competitive swimming, going to the gym, traveling and preparing his kids for success in their future endeavors.
